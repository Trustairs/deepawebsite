Deepa has developed two original frameworks that are redefining how organizations approach ESG governance and AI accountability. Download the playbooks and join the conversation shaping the future of responsible business.

TRUST
The TRUST Framework
Sustainability Reporting Governance

A five-pillar board-level governance model for sustainability reporting. TRUST provides the structural foundation for organizations to govern their ESG disclosures with rigour, transparency, and accountability — ensuring data integrity from collection to publication.

T
Transparency
Full visibility into data sources, assumptions, and methodologies behind ESG disclosures.
R
Responsibility
Clear governance roles and accountability chains from board to operational teams.
U
Understanding
Board sustainability literacy — ensuring leadership can interrogate and act on ESG data.
S
Security & Sustainability
Information system integrity underpinning reliable, audit-ready ESG reporting.
T
Trustworthiness
Independent validation enabling investors and stakeholders to rely on disclosures.

AIRS
The AIRS Framework
AI Governance in Corporate Reporting

A four-pillar model for governing AI-influenced disclosures in corporate reporting. AIRS addresses the critical gap in how organizations manage, disclose, and take accountability for AI's role in their sustainability and financial reporting processes.

A
Accountability
Clear ownership of AI-influenced outputs across the reporting lifecycle — from model to disclosure.
I
Integrity
Ensuring AI-generated data maintains accuracy, consistency, and freedom from bias.
R
Responsibility
Ethical deployment of AI tools and evolving regulatory standards.
S
Sustainability
Long-term traceability and auditability of AI processes embedded in corporate reporting.
